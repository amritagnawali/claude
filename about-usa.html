Study in the United States

Home to the largest number of international students in the world, with flexible academic programmes and unmatched research opportunities.

The United States hosts more international students than any other country, offering an unmatched range of universities, majors and campus experiences. From the flexibility to explore subjects before declaring a major to some of the world's leading research institutions, the US remains a top choice for Nepali students pursuing higher education.

Capital Washington, D.C.
Language English
Currency US Dollar ($)
Popular Cities New York, Boston, Los Angeles
Why United States

Why Study in the United States?

Reasons thousands of Nepali students choose the United States for their higher education

Wide Range of Programmes

Thousands of accredited universities and colleges offering nearly every field of study imaginable.

Research & Innovation

Access to cutting-edge research facilities and world-leading faculty across STEM, business and the arts.

Flexible Curriculum

The credit system lets students explore different subjects before committing to a major.

Optional Practical Training (OPT)

Eligible F-1 students can gain up to a year (longer for STEM fields) of practical US work experience after graduating.

Diverse Campus Life

Large, active international student communities and student organisations on nearly every campus.

Globally Respected Degrees

A US degree carries strong recognition with employers worldwide.
