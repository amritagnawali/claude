A Message From Our CEO

[Sunil Nepal]

Founder & CEO, Incept Education Consultancy

Dear students and parents, it gives me immense pride to welcome you to Incept Education Consultancy. When we started this journey over 15 years ago, our goal was simple — to make world-class education accessible to every deserving student in Nepal, regardless of their background.

Today, having guided thousands of students to universities across the UK, USA, Australia, Germany, and New Zealand, I can say with confidence that our success is measured not in numbers, but in the dreams we've helped turn into reality.

Studying abroad is one of the most important decisions of your life, and our entire team is committed to standing beside you at every step — from choosing the right course to settling into your new home overseas. We don't just process applications; we build futures.

I invite you to visit our office, meet our counselors, and let us be part of your journey towards a brighter, global future.

[Sunil Nepal]
Founder & CEO
