🇳🇿Study in the New Zealand

A safe, welcoming country with a quality-assured education system and stunning natural surroundings.

New Zealand offers a high standard of education in a relaxed, safe and welcoming environment. With every one of its universities internationally ranked and a strong focus on practical, research-based learning, it's an increasingly popular choice for Nepali students looking for a smaller, close-knit study experience.

Capital Wellington
Language English

Why New Zealand

Why Study in the New Zealand?

Reasons thousands of Nepali students choose the New Zealand for their higher education

Quality-Assured Education

All eight New Zealand universities are internationally ranked, with consistent quality across the country.

Safe & Welcoming

Consistently ranked among the safest countries in the world, with a relaxed, friendly lifestyle.

Work While You Study

Eligible students can generally work part-time during the semester and full-time during scheduled breaks.

Post-Study Work Visa

Many graduates are eligible for a post-study work visa, opening the door to valuable local work experience.

Quality of Life

Stunning natural landscapes and a relaxed pace of life alongside a modern education system.

Globally Recognised Qualifications

Degrees are respected by employers across the Asia-Pacific region and beyond.
