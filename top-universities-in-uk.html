Top 20 Universities in the UK for Nepali Students

A closer look at 20 UKVI-approved universities our counsellors regularly recommend, spanning London, the North of England, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

Back to Study in UK
1

University of Sunderland

Campuses in Sunderland and London, high graduate employability, and popular programs in Data Science, MBA, Cybersecurity and Nursing. One of the more affordable UK cities to live in.

2

Coventry University

Over 300 courses across Coventry, London and Scarborough, recognised for international student diversity and strong industry links in Business, Engineering and Design.

3

University of Roehampton

A green, college-style campus in southwest London with smaller class sizes. Strong in Psychology, Business, Education and Sports Science, with well-regarded international student support.

4

Ravensbourne University London

A specialist creative university on Greenwich Peninsula with strong industry connections in digital media, fashion, broadcasting and UX design.

5

York St John University

A personal, tutor-led university experience in the historic city of York, with programs in Business, Education, Health, Media and the Arts.

6

Ulster University

Campuses across Northern Ireland plus London, Birmingham and Manchester, offering flexibility to move between cities during or after study.

7

University of the West of Scotland

Campuses across Scotland and in London, with career-focused programs in Business, Computing, Nursing and Engineering and a lower cost of living than the capital.

8

University of East London

Based in Stratford and Docklands, ranked highly for graduate employability improvement, with strong Architecture, Nursing, Psychology and Business programs.

9

BPP University

A profession-first university specialising in Law, Business, Technology and Nursing, with study centres across England including London, Manchester and Leeds.

10

University of West London

Based in Ealing near Heathrow, well regarded for Nursing, Healthcare, Business, Music and Hospitality Management, with strong NHS and hospitality career outcomes.

11

University College Birmingham

A specialist institution for Hospitality, Tourism, Culinary Arts, Business and Sport, in a city with a well-established Nepali and South Asian community.

12

University of Wolverhampton

A community-focused university in the West Midlands with strong pastoral support, offering Business, Law, Computing, Health Sciences and Engineering.

13

University of Worcester

A peaceful, affordable campus city with very high student satisfaction scores, strong in Education, Health Sciences, Business and Sports Science.

14

University of Central Lancashire

One of the largest UK universities, based in Preston, offering 500+ courses and one of the lowest costs of living of any university city.

15

University of Huddersfield

Named University of the Year by Times Higher Education, with standout teaching quality and one of the most budget-friendly student living costs in the UK.

16

London South Bank University

A STEM-strong university in Zone 1 London, popular for Nursing, Civil Engineering, Computing, Architecture and Business.

17

London Metropolitan University

One of the most diverse universities in the world, with accessible tuition fees and campuses in Islington and Aldgate, central London.

18

Leeds Trinity University

A close-knit, teaching-focused university in Leeds, strong in Journalism, Media, Education, Business and Sports, with a lower cost of living than London.

19

University of the West of England (UWE Bristol)

A leading modern university in innovative, multicultural Bristol, strong in Engineering, Robotics, Business, Law and Health Sciences.

20

De Montfort University

A vibrant campus in diverse Leicester, career-focused programs in Business, Art and Design, Engineering, Law and Health Sciences, with a lower cost of living than London.
