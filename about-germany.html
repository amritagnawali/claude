🇩🇪Study in the Germany

A leading destination for engineering and technical education, with many public universities charging little to no tuition fees.

Germany has become one of the most attractive destinations for Nepali students thanks to its low-cost (often tuition-free) public universities, strong engineering and technical programmes, and central location in Europe. It is an especially popular choice for students pursuing STEM subjects.

Capital Berlin
Language German (many English-taught programmes)
Currency Euro (€)
Popular Cities Berlin, Munich, Frankfurt
Why Germany

Why Study in the Germany?

Reasons thousands of Nepali students choose the Germany for their higher education

Low / No Tuition Fees

Most public universities charge little to no tuition for undergraduate and many postgraduate programmes.

Strong in Engineering & Technology

Renowned for engineering, automotive, and applied sciences programmes with strong industry links.

Work While You Study

Students are generally permitted to work part-time during the semester to support living costs.

Post-Study Work Opportunities

Graduates can typically apply for an extended residence permit to search for a job related to their qualification.

Central European Location

Easy, affordable travel to explore the rest of Europe during breaks.

Strong Global Economy

A degree from Germany is well regarded, particularly in engineering, business and applied sciences.
